# Personas

A Persona is a logical container that groups related Skills by role or function. For example, an SRE Persona combines troubleshooting, monitoring, and incident response skills, while a Provisioning Persona bundles Terraform and Kubernetes deployment skills, and a Security Persona groups compliance and security scanning skills.



To create a Persona, follow these steps:&#x20;



**Step 1:** Click on Personas from the left menu and click on Add Persona. <br>

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/unknown (10).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>



**Step 2:** Select all the relevant skills for this Persona and hit Update.
