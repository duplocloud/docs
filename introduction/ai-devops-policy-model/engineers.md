# Engineers

You’re now ready to assemble your AI engineer by combining providers, skills/personas, and agents.



**Step 1:** Begin by navigating to the Engineers section from left menu and clicking the Add Engineer button

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/unknown (13).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

<br>

**Step 2:** Name your engineer and describe their responsibilities. Attach the right Persona to equip the engineer with all needed skills. Click Next.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/unknown (14).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>



**Step 3:** Select agents that you want this engineer to use to assign specific project tasks. Click Next.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/unknown (15).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Step 4:** Select providers and their respective scopes for the engineer you wish to create.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/unknown (16).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>



**Step 5:** Complete the process by clicking Create.&#x20;





**Congratulations!** Your engineer is now ready to receive assignments, interpret goals, break down work, and provide a transparent audit trail—all with human oversight and control.

Adding an AI engineer streamlines DevOps while keeping you in control. Assign projects using natural language, and let your digital teammate handle the technical details and implementation.
